# Generative AI

This track is organized as a proper curriculum instead of one flat topic list. The goal is to take you from first principles to production-grade GenAI systems and interview-level mastery.

## What this track is trying to build

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

- explain how LLMs work at a practical systems level
- choose between prompting, RAG, fine-tuning, and agent workflows
- build document-grounded and tool-using AI applications
- reason about hallucinations, latency, safety, evaluation, and cost
- discuss training, inference, multimodality, and deployment in interviews

## Recommended study flow

1. Start with the foundations group.
2. Move into prompting and retrieval after you understand tokens, transformers, and inference.
3. Learn evaluation and safety before building more advanced systems.
4. Study agents, multimodal systems, and LLMOps after the core patterns are clear.
5. Finish with interviews and projects so the ideas turn into practical skill.
